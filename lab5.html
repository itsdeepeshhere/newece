Digital Signal Processing and Embeded System Lab

DIGITAL SIGNAL PROCESSING LAB and Embedded Engineering | ECE

Information

DIGITAL SIGNAL PROCESSING LAB has been created to strengthen the facilities in the thrust areaDigital Signal Processing. The Laboratory is grown and developed in view of carrying out DSP based projects in the areas of signal Processing, Speech Signal Processing, Image Processing, Communication and Bio-Medical Signal Processing. In this regard, the Laboratory is being facilitated with adequate Hardware as well as Software support to carry out the same. Students are made acquainted with the knowledge of Digital Signal Processing, DSP devices

Embedded Engineering Is constantly upgraded with new equipments. More design oriented experiments are being offered in laboratory classes. Lab manuals have been prepared for all the laboratories. Based on the technological innovations, new facilities are being added every year for providing training to the students to make them suitable for the future needs. Students are made acquainted with the basic fundamentals embedded system.
