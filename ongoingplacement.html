Ongoing Placements

The departmental Internship Advisory Committee along with TPO help the students in arranging their training in the industry. We train students to meet the requirements of a firm in order to prepare them for employment.

Placement 2018-2022 (ongoing)

Campus placements provide several opportunities for students to demonstrate their abilities, which many firms desire to explore and employ. We train students to meet the requirements of a firm in order to prepare them for employment. Every year, reputable companies, including multinational corporations, visit to campus to recruit. We host technical presentations and national seminars to give aspiring engineers a chance to network with professionals from a variety of businesses.

Placement Details
  • In this tough time of pandemic, Department of Electronics & Communication excelled with more than 90% of placements along with more than 50% of students having double offers with 10% of students having triple offers with an overall average CTC of 15 LPA including highest 52 LPA CTC and 44 LPA CTC of Atlassian and Amazon respectively. In case of 6 Month internships More than 70% of students got opportunities in working in corporate before even their degree finishes with the highest stipend of 87,000 per month at Morgan Stanley.
  • Going brief inside insights every engineering student wish to work for FAANG (Facebook Amazon Apple Netflix Google) and our 12 students got opportunity to work for Amazon at whopping CTC of 44 LPA along with 80,000 per month stipend for intern along with one student got an opportunity at Atlassian with CTC of 52 LPA to work over the products which each IT company uses very rigorously. Dept of ECE also made one offer from World's top fortune company Walmart with an intern stipend of 85,000 per month./li>
  • This year our students also entered the hardware industry by vouching in one of top notch companies including Nvidia & Texas Instruments.
  • Our 10 students also made a place in the BIG FOUR of the world (Deloitte & KPMG), Along with 8 students in one growing payment app (PayTM) and 2 students in FinTech (American Express (AmEx)).
  • More than 25% of students of the whole ECE got offers from SAMSUNG (Samsung Data, Samsung Research Bangalore, Samsung Research Noida). Our students are also ready to mix the technology in the Pharma world; 10 students got offers from two big names in pharma i.e Optum and ZS along with 16 offers from Oracle (GBU, OFSS).
  • For pre-final year, About 20% students got an internship offer of 2 months from Microsoft, Standard Chartered Bank, Samsung, Accenture, Texas Instruments with the highest intern stipend of 80,000 per month by microsoft.
