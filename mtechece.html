Program Educational Objectives

PEO 1

Postgraduates would be competent, creative, and highly valued professionals in industry, academia, or government

PEO 2

Postgraduates shall be able to integrate foundation in engineering and in depth knowledge so as toÂ use it for contemporary problem solving in the analysis, design, and evaluation of electronic devices, electronic systems, communication and related systems
