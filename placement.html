PLACEMENT

This era of globalization has created enormous opportunities for engineers in all the walks of life. As a result, young engineers are expected to easily integrate into the global business culture. Campus placements provide several opportunities for students to demonstrate their abilities, which many firms desire to explore and employ. We train students to meet the requirements of a firm in order to prepare them for employment. Every year, reputable companies, including multinational corporations, visit to campus to recruit. We host technical presentations and national seminars to give aspiring engineers a chance to network with professionals from a variety of businesses.

During 8 th semester of B Tech, 50% students are allowed to undergo 6-months Industrial Internship through on campus mode. The departmental Internship Advisory Committee along with TPO help the students in arranging their training in the industry. MTech students are allowed to undergo 1 year Internship for their thesis work during the second year of their PG Program.
