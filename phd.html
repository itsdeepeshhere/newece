How to get into PhD
    • NIT Jalandhar offers Ph.D. (Full Time / Part Time) in various disciplines of physics, for which applications are invited twice a year through an advertisement in the national dailies, media and on the institute's website.
    • All the eligible candidates have to appear for an Entrance Test and those, who secure minimum 40% marks in this test, shall be called for the interview to be organized by the respective department.
    • The candidates possessing minimum 60% marks (or CGPA 6.5/10) in the master’s degree along with qualified NET / GATE or any equivalent external fellowship are eligible to apply for the Full Time Ph.D. programme. However, for Part Time Ph.D., candidates specified by industry/ institute having R&D facility recognized by NITJ preferably with two years’ experience and having the same academic qualification as that for PhD regular candidates are eligible to apply.
    • At the time of interview, candidates are expected to discuss their research interest/area of interest.
    • Keeping in view the available seats, the Interview Committee shall recommend the names of candidates found suitable for admission to Ph.D. Programme for the approval of Chairman, Senate. The Associate Dean Academics (PG)/Dean (Academics) shall notify the list of selected candidates through appropriate means.
