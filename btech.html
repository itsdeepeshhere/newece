Program Educational Objectives

PEO 1

To provide strong background in basic sciences, mathematics, computing and engineering principles

PEO 2

To impart in-depth knowledge to students related to core areas of Electronics and Communication Engineering so as to comprehend, analyze, design, and create novel products and solutions for real life problems

PEO 3

To provide students with an academic environment to promote teamwork, ethics, multidisciplinary approach and lifelong learning required for a successful professional career

Program Outcomes
  • Apply knowledge of mathematics, science, and engineering fundamentals in the domain of Electronics and Communication
  • Potential to analyze an engineering problem and formulate its appropriate solution
  • Ability to design systems and processes that meet the requirements of public safety and offer solutions for societal and environmental issues
  • Ability to formulate and analyze complex engineering problems by using mathematical principles and engineering fundamentals
  • Select appropriate techniques and modern automation tools for the system design and analysis
  • Understanding the contemporary issues and the impact of engineering solutions on the society
  • Skills to develop environment friendly and sustainable solutions
  • Understanding and commitment towards professional ethics, responsibilities and norms of engineering practices so as to become good citizens
  • Ability to function effectively, individually and in a team
  • Proficiency in communication, both verbal and written forms, which will enable them to compete globally
  • Recognize the need for and have the ability to engage in independent and lifelong learning and hence participate and succeed in competitive examinations, higher studies etc.
  • Willingness and ability to take up administrative responsibilities involving both project and financial management confidently
